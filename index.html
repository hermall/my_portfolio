About Me

I am a 2022 graduate of the Tippie College of Business at the University of Iowa with a degree in Business Analytics and Information Systems, a certificate in Interscholastic Athletic/Activity Administration, and a minor in Sport and Recreation Management. I have been involved with aquatics since I started swimming competitively at the age of 8. In high school, I was the swim team manager and was responsible for all timing equipment. That passion for aquatics grew at the University of Iowa where I am an advocate for sport equality, a lead coach for our club team, and an aquatic equipment technician for all aquatic events on campus.
